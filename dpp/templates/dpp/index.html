A few words about the 'Doctor Patient Portal'

The DPP system is designed to bridge the gap between doctors and their patients. It provides a wide range of functionalities, patients can easily book appointments and they also have have access to their doctor of choice's schedule. Doctors get access/store patient important information with just a few clicks and more...
