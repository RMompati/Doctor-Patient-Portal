Few Words About DPP

Doctor Patient Portal, is a system designed to bridge the gap between doctors and patients. Appointments can be booked online, a doctor updates his/her schedule regularly so that respective patients can chose appropriate time slots and dates when booking an appointment. It also provides a wide range of functionalities, a doctor can view a patient medical records with just a few clicks, storing patient diagnosis and information is easy, and more..
